About

HomeChoice NSW website visualises sales and rent pay data based on NSW suburbs renting and house price trends data released by NSW houshing.

  • All statistics were sourced from Rent and sales Report- Issue 119.
  • This will facilitate Sydney -siders to quickly view the renting and selling data within different suburb and time period
  • All data was aggregated on state and occupation group level.
  • Our aim is to make the data more friendly and interesting with beautiful experience.
Flash Player is needed for viewing Graphs
